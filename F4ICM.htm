Notification | Schedule | Photo

Welcome to the Forum

The Forum for Intelligent Computer Mathematics aims to provide an opportunity to immerse experts and scholars from the field of computer mathematics in the exploration of fundamental and cutting-edge techniques in the realm of artificial intelligence, symbolic computation, and automated reasoning. This forum is designed to provide a platform for researchers across the country engaged in computer mathematics to summarize and exchange the latest research findings in domestic and international computer mathematics, with a particular emphasis on current tracks and intelligent methodologies.
The forum themes include, but are not limited to, the following research directions:

  • Mechanization theory, algorithms, and applications in mathematics;
  • Symbolic computation, and the hybridization of symbolic and numerical computation;
  • Mathematical foundations of artificial intelligence, automated reasoning, and machine proofing;
  • Applications of computer mathematics in information security, program verification, robotics, and numerical control systems;
  • Mathematical methods in high-tech fields such as computer graphics, image processing, and pattern recognition;
  • Advanced quantum algorithms;
  • Intelligent mathematical software that utilizes AI techniques.
